A simple GitScrum plugin for Wordpress. You will be able to manage your projects without having to leave Wordpress.

* This is an MVP version of the plugin.
** A minimum viable product, or MVP, is a product with enough features to attract early-adopter customers and validate a product idea early in the product development cycle.

### What is GitScrum?
GitScrum is a Project Management Tool, developed to help entrepreneurs, freelancers, managers, and teams Skyrocket their Productivity with the Agile methodology and Gamification.

Our objective is help teams to create, plan, organize and release amazing projects.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Everything was thought for Skyrocket your Digital Agency!  =

Exceed client expectations and deliver consistently with a project-client management platform that will allow your agency to grow without limitations.

= What does Workspaces mean? =

Workspaces were created to make it easier for your digital agency's clients to organize. We suggest that you create a Workspace for each client, so you can better define teams and all client-related projects.
